Non-UK Born Dwellers

  • In 2001, 8.3% of UK dwellers were non-UK born

  • In 2015, the number of non-UK born dwellers rose to 13.3%

Immigration is a hot topic, and yet hardly anyone understands the true nature of the issue, and how it differs across regions. This tool is here to dispel the myths.

Works for postcodes in England and Wales (Scotland and Northern Ireland do not collect accurate enough data. Sorry!)

The area with the highest proportion of residents not born in the UK is Newham, East London, where in 2015 54.1% of residents were born outside the UK.

The area with the lowest proportion of residents not born in the UK is Rhondda Cynon Taff, South Wales, where in 2015 2.12% of residents were born outside the UK.

Type in the first part of your postcode, e.g. AB1, to find out how your local area compares to the national average of non-UK born dwellers.
